ASTRAVAULT WHITEPAPER

Local Asset Utility Infrastructure

Version 1.0

Summary

AstraVault routes stable digital assets into local commerce categories and settles merchant payouts through transparent wallets.

Core Model

Deposits are allocated to category modules (clothing, services, and balanced baskets). Each module maintains reserve liquidity to protect checkout continuity.

Governance

Allocation parameters can be updated by governance votes and city-level merchant advisory groups.

Roadmap

Phase 1: pilot cities and merchant tools. Phase 2: pooled liquidity and on-chain proofs. Phase 3: regional expansion.
